<p><p>Robot grippers are essential components in automated systems, designed to handle various materials. Electric grippers use motors for precise control and are ideal for delicate tasks. Pneumatic grippers utilize compressed air for strong, quick gripping, making them suitable for high-speed operations. Vacuum grippers, or suction cups, use suction to lift flat or non-porous objects, excelling in handling lightweight materials. Magnetic grippers employ magnets to secure ferrous items, offering a reliable solution for metal parts. Each type serves specific industrial applications.</p></p>
