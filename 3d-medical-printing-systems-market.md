<p><p>The 3D medical printing systems market comprises several key technologies: </p><p>1. Stereolithography (SLA) utilizes UV light to cure liquid resin into solid parts, offering high-resolution precision for intricate designs. </p><p>2. Digital Light Processing (DLP) is similar but uses a digital light projector, enhancing speed through layer exposure. </p><p>3. Fused Deposition Modeling (FDM) extrudes thermoplastic materials, favoring cost-effectiveness and versatility. </p><p>4. Selective Laser Sintering (SLS) employs lasers to fuse powdered materials, producing durable, complex geometries. </p><p>5. Electron Beam Melting (EBM) utilizes high-energy electron beams for metal components, ideal for aerospace-grade implants. </p><p>6. Other methods encompass various emerging technologies and specialized applications.</p></p>
