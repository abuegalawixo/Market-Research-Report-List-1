<p><p>Conditioning agents in hair formulations enhance the texture, manageability, and overall appearance of hair. STMS (Stearamidopropyl dimethylamine) offers detangling benefits. CTMS (Cetrimonium methosulfate) acts as a conditioning surfactant. SAPDA and BAPDA are silicone derivatives that add shine and moisture. Other agents may include natural alternatives and specialty compounds catering to specific hair care needs.</p></p>
