<p><strong>The Instrument Landing System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Instrument Landing System CAT I</li><li>Instrument Landing System CAT II</li><li>Instrument Landing System CAT III</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Instrument Landing System (ILS) market is categorized into three types based on precision and operational conditions. ILS CAT I provides basic approach guidance, requiring a decision height of at least 200 feet. ILS CAT II improves reliability and allows landing with decision heights as low as 100 feet, enhancing safety in low visibility. ILS CAT III offers the highest precision, enabling landings with decision heights near zero, crucial for airports with frequent poor weather conditions, thus expanding accessibility and operational efficiency.</p></p>
