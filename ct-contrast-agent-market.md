<p><p>The CT contrast agent market is primarily categorized into iodine-based and barium sulfate compounds. Iodine-based contrast agents are widely used for enhanced imaging during computed tomography scans, offering excellent visibility of vascular structures and soft tissues. They can be administered intravenously or orally. Barium sulfate compounds, on the other hand, are mainly utilized for gastrointestinal imaging, helping to visualize the digestive tract. Both types play crucial roles in diagnostics, offering distinct advantages depending on the targeted area of the body.</p></p>
