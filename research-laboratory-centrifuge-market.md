<p><strong>The Research Laboratory Centrifuge Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vertical</li><li>Horizontal</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The research laboratory centrifuge market is segmented mainly into vertical and horizontal types. Vertical centrifuges have a rotor that remains in a fixed position, allowing for effective pelleting of samples and easier access to supernatant. They are ideal for quick sedimentation and are often used in clinical applications. In contrast, horizontal centrifuges have rotors that spin in a horizontal plane, promoting greater separation efficiency and sample clarity, making them suitable for molecular and cell biology applications.</p></p>
