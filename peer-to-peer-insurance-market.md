<p><strong>The Peer to Peer Insurance Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Broker</li><li>Carrier</li><li>Stealth</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Peer-to-peer insurance operates in three main market types: </p><p>1. **Broker Model**: This involves a platform that connects individuals, facilitating group insurance by pooling resources among peers, while retaining traditional brokers' roles. </p><p>2. **Carrier Model**: In this framework, a new insurer directly underwrites the pooled premium, handling claims and payouts within the community.</p><p>3. **Stealth Market**: Here, peer-to-peer transactions occur without formal recognition as insurance, relying on mutual agreements among individuals for shared risks, often fostering trust and community engagement.</p></p>
