<p><p>The LVDT sensor market is primarily categorized into DC-operated and AC-operated sensors. DC-operated LVDTs utilize direct current to produce a steady output voltage, providing high accuracy and stability, making them suitable for applications requiring precise measurements. In contrast, AC-operated LVDTs use alternating current, offering advantages in terms of noise immunity and robustness, making them ideal for harsh industrial environments. Both types cater to various applications, including aerospace, automotive, and manufacturing, enhancing measurement reliability in diverse settings.</p></p>
