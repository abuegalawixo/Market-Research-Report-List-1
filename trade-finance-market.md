<p><p>Trade finance encompasses various market types that facilitate international transactions. Letters of Credit ensure payment security to sellers, while Guarantees provide assurance against buyer defaults. Supply Chain Finance optimizes cash flow between buyers and suppliers by advancing payments. Documentary Collection allows banks to manage the exchange of documents for payment, minimizing risk. Other market types include factoring, where receivables are sold for immediate cash, and forfaiting, involving the purchase of medium to long-term receivables to enhance liquidity.</p></p>
