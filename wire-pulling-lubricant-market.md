<p><p>The wire-pulling lubricant market can be categorized into three main types: liquid, wax, and gel. Liquid lubricants provide excellent flow and coverage, making them ideal for high-speed applications. Wax lubricants offer a more solid consistency, providing better adherence and reducing friction over time, suitable for longer pulls. Gel lubricants combine the benefits of both liquid and wax, offering a thick consistency that ensures effective lubrication while preventing drips, making them versatile for various wire-pulling scenarios.</p></p>
