<p><ul><li>Contact Voltage Tester</li><li>Non-contact Voltage Tester</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The voltage tester market includes contact and non-contact voltage testers, each serving distinct purposes. Contact voltage testers require direct contact with a live circuit to measure voltage levels accurately, making them suitable for in-depth diagnostics. In contrast, non-contact voltage testers detect electric fields from a distance, offering a safer and quicker way to identify live wires without direct contact. Both types are essential for electricians and maintenance professionals, ensuring safety and efficiency in electrical work.</p></p>
