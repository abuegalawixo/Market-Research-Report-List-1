<p><p>The water knife cutting machine market is primarily segmented into two types: sand water knives and pure water knives. Sand water knives utilize a mixture of water and abrasive materials, making them suitable for cutting hard materials like metals and stone with high precision. In contrast, pure water knives rely solely on high-pressure water streams, ideal for softer materials such as food and plastics, offering clean cuts without contamination. Both types cater to diverse industries, enhancing cutting efficiency and accuracy.</p></p>
