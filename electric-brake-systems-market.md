<p><ul><li>Disc EBS</li><li>Drum EBS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric brake systems (EBS) market is primarily divided into two types: disc EBS and drum EBS. Disc EBS utilizes disc brake technology, offering superior performance and heat dissipation, making them ideal for high-speed applications and modern vehicles. In contrast, drum EBS employs a traditional drum brake mechanism, often favored in heavy-duty trucks due to their robust design and reliability under heavy loads. Both systems enhance braking efficiency and safety by providing precise control and responsiveness in vehicle braking operations.</p></p>
