<p><p>The Extended Wear Contact Lenses market is segmented into three main types: Spherical, Toric, and Multifocal forms. Spherical contact lenses correct nearsightedness or farsightedness and maintain a uniform curvature. Toric lenses are designed for astigmatism, featuring varying powers in different meridians for precise vision correction. Multifocal lenses cater to presbyopia, offering multiple prescriptions within a single lens to aid clear vision at various distances. Each type is tailored to specific vision needs, enhancing comfort and convenience for users.</p></p>
