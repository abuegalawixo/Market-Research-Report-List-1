<p><p>Thromboelastography (TEG) is a diagnostic tool used to assess the coagulation properties of blood in a comprehensive manner. It measures the viscoelastic properties of whole blood as it undergoes clot formation, stabilization, and breakdown, providing valuable insights into various clotting disorders and guiding anticoagulation therapy. The TEG machine's ability to deliver real-time results makes it essential in surgical settings, trauma management, and critical care.</p><p>The Thromboelastography (TEG) Machine Market is poised for significant growth, driven by an increasing emphasis on personalized medicine and the rapid expansion of surgical procedures requiring precise coagulation monitoring. Innovations in TEG technology, such as automated systems and integration with electronic health records, are further enhancing their utility in clinical practice. Additionally, the rising prevalence of coagulopathy and the growing geriatric population are contributing factors influencing market dynamics. The market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.6% during the forecast period.
