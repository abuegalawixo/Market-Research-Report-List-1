<p><p>The ultrasonic coating market features two primary types: rotary-type and flat-type ultrasonic coating systems. Rotary-type systems utilize a rotating substrate, allowing for uniform coating application through consistent ultrasonic energy. This method is ideal for cylindrical objects and provides efficient coverage. In contrast, flat-type ultrasonic coating systems are designed for flat surfaces, delivering precise coatings on various substrates such as films and sheets. Both systems leverage ultrasonic technology to enhance coating adhesion and quality, catering to diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
