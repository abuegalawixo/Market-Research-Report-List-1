<p><p>Yeasts, yeast extracts, and autolysates are essential components in various industries, including food and beverages, pharmaceuticals, and nutraceuticals. Yeasts are unicellular fungi known for their fermentation properties, widely used in baking, brewing, and winemaking. Yeast extracts, derived from the breakdown of yeast cells, are valued for their flavor-enhancing properties and nutritional content, commonly utilized in soups, sauces, and snacks. Autolysates are produced through the enzymatic breakdown of yeast, resulting in a rich source of amino acids, vitamins, and minerals, making them beneficial for food industry applications.</p><p>The market for yeasts, yeast extracts, and autolysates is experiencing significant growth, driven by the rising demand for natural flavoring agents, the increasing popularity of plant-based diets, and the expanding utilization of these products in the pharmaceutical sector for their health benefits.
