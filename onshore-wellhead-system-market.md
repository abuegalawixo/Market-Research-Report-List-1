<p><strong>The Onshore Wellhead System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional Wellhead System</li><li>Multi-bowl Wellhead System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Onshore Wellhead System market encompasses two primary types: Conventional Wellhead Systems and Multi-bowl Wellhead Systems. Conventional Wellhead Systems are typically used in simpler drilling operations, offering a single bowl configuration to manage reservoir pressures and fluids. In contrast, Multi-bowl Wellhead Systems provide enhanced flexibility and safety for complex well conditions by incorporating multiple bowl designs, allowing for better management of various wellbore segments. The choice between these systems depends on operational requirements and geological challenges.</p></p>
