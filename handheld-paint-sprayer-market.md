<p><p>The handheld paint sprayer market comprises several types, including airless, HVLP (high volume low pressure), and others. Airless sprayers use high pressure to atomize paint, allowing for fast application on large surfaces. HVLP sprayers operate at lower pressure, producing a fine mist for precise control and reduced overspray, making them ideal for detailed work. Other types may include pneumatic and battery-operated models, catering to various user preferences and project requirements, enhancing versatility and efficiency in painting tasks.</p></p>
