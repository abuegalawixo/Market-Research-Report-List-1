<p><p>The electric transmission oil pumps market is segmented into two main types: integrated pumps and separate pumps. Integrated pumps are built directly into the transmission system, providing efficient oil circulation and reducing space requirements. In contrast, separate pumps are standalone units that can be installed independently, allowing for greater flexibility in design and maintenance. Both types serve the critical function of ensuring optimal lubrication and cooling of the transmission components, enhancing performance and longevity of vehicles.</p></p>
