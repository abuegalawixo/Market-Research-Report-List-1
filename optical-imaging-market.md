<p><p>The optical imaging market comprises various advanced technologies used for medical diagnostics and research. Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT) provides high-resolution imaging of biological tissues, particularly in ophthalmology. Photoacoustic Imaging combines optical and ultrasound techniques for enhanced tissue visualization. Diffused Optical Tomography utilizes light to image the deep tissue structure, while Hyperspectral Imaging captures a wide spectrum of light to analyze materials and detect disease. Near-Infrared Spectroscopy analyzes light absorption to assess tissue composition and function, aiding in non-invasive diagnostics.</p></p>
