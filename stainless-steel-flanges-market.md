<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Stainless Steel Flanges Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Weld Flange</li><li>Blind Flange</li><li>Slip-On Flange</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stainless steel flanges market includes various types, each serving specific purposes in piping systems. Weld flanges are designed to be welded to pipes for strong, permanent connections. Blind flanges seal off the end of a pipe, preventing flow while allowing access for maintenance. Slip-on flanges easily slide over pipes and are welded in place, providing versatility. Other flanges can include threaded, socket-weld, and lap joint designs, catering to diverse industrial applications and connection requirements.</p></p>
