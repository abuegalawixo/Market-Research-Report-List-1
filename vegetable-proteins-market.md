<p><p>The vegetable proteins market is divided into two main types: complete and incomplete proteins. In contrast, incomplete proteins lack one or more of these amino acids and are typically derived from legumes, nuts, and grains. Consumers increasingly seek plant-based protein sources for health, sustainability, and dietary preferences, driving growth in both categories within the market.</p></p>
