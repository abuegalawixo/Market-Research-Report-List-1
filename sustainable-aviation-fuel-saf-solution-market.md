<p><p>Sustainable Aviation Fuel (SAF) encompasses two primary market types: Biological SAF and Synthetic SAF. Biological SAF is derived from renewable resources such as agricultural residues, waste oils, and biomass, aiming to minimize carbon emissions by utilizing existing waste streams. In contrast, Synthetic SAF is produced through chemical processes using carbon capture and renewable energy, capable of replicating conventional jet fuel's properties while significantly reducing lifecycle emissions. Both types are crucial in advancing the aviation industry's sustainability goals.</p></p>
