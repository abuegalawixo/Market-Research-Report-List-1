<p><ul><li>Cage Mountable</li><li>Post Mountable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fixed optical mount market primarily encompasses two types: cage mountable and post mountable systems. Cage mountable designs integrate with optical cages, providing a flexible and modular setup for aligning optics within a framework. In contrast, post mountable systems are designed to attach directly to optical posts, offering robust stability and ease of adjustment. Both types cater to diverse applications in laboratories and industrial settings, emphasizing precision, alignment, and versatility in optical setups for various research and engineering purposes.</p></p>
