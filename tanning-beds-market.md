<p><p>The tanning bed market is divided into three main types: UVA tanning beds, UVB tanning beds, and combination tanning beds. UVA tanning beds primarily emit long-wave ultraviolet A rays, promoting a deeper tan with lower risk of sunburn. UVB tanning beds, on the other hand, emit shorter-wave ultraviolet B rays, which are effective for quicker tanning but can increase the likelihood of skin damage. Combination tanning beds offer both UVA and UVB rays, providing a balanced tanning experience for users.</p></p>
