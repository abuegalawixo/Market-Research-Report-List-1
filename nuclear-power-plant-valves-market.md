<p><p>The nuclear power plant valves market encompasses various types essential for operational efficiency and safety. Shut-off valves control fluid flow in emergency situations, while butterfly valves enable quick throttling and are lightweight. Diaphragm valves provide tight sealing for corrosive fluids, ensuring integrity, and ball valves offer reliable sealing with minimal flow resistance.
