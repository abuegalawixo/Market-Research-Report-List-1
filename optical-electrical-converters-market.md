<p><ul><li>Multi-Mode</li><li>Single Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical-electrical converters market features two primary types: multi-mode and single-mode converters. Multi-mode converters utilize multiple light paths, enabling higher data rates over short distances, making them ideal for local area networks and data centers. In contrast, single-mode converters use a single light path for longer distances, offering greater bandwidth and lower signal loss, suitable for telecommunications and long-distance data transmission. Each type caters to specific applications based on distance, bandwidth requirements, and network infrastructure.</p></p>
