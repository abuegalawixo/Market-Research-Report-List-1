<p><p>The SiC polishing equipment market is categorized into two main types: single-sided polishing and double-sided polishing. Single-sided polishing equipment is designed to polish one side of a substrate, typically offering precision for specific applications. In contrast, double-sided polishing equipment simultaneously polishes both sides, increasing efficiency and throughput, making it suitable for mass production. Both types serve critical roles in semiconductor fabrication, enhancing surface quality and ensuring the optimal performance of silicon carbide materials in various electronic applications.</p></p>
