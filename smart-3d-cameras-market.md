<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Smart 3D Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Binocular Stereo Vision</li><li>Time of Flight</li><li>Structured Light</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Smart 3D Cameras market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. **Binocular Stereo Vision**: This technology employs two lenses to capture depth information by comparing the images from each lens, mimicking human vision to assess distance.</p><p>2. **Time of Flight (ToF)**: ToF cameras measure the time taken by light pulses to travel to an object and back, creating accurate depth maps for real-time imaging.</p><p>3. **Structured Light**: This method projects a known pattern onto a scene and analyzes the deformation of that pattern to calculate depth, allowing for high-resolution 3D capture.</p></p>
