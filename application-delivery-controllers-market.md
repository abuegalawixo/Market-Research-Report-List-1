<p><p>Application Delivery Controllers (ADCs) play a vital role in managing application traffic for improved performance and security. The market is divided into two main types: hardware-based and virtual ADCs. Hardware-based ADCs are dedicated appliances offering high performance, reliability, and specialized resources tailored for large enterprises. In contrast, virtual ADCs operate as software instances on virtual machines, providing flexibility and scalability, making them ideal for cloud environments and small to medium-sized businesses seeking cost-effective solutions.</p></p>
