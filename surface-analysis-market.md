<p><p>Surface analysis encompasses various market types including microscopy, spectroscopy, surface analyzers, and X-ray diffraction (XRD). Microscopy techniques like electron and atomic force microscopy provide detailed images of surfaces at the nanoscale. Spectroscopy methods analyze surface composition and chemical properties. Surface analyzers assess characteristics such as roughness and morphology. X-ray diffraction (XRD) evaluates crystalline structures and phase compositions. Together, these techniques enable comprehensive understanding of material properties, aiding advancements in fields such as nanotechnology, materials science, and electronics.</p></p>
