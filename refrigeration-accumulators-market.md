<p><p>Refrigeration accumulators are essential for managing refrigerant flow and enhancing system efficiency. The market features several types: </p><p>1. **Piston Accumulators** use a movable piston to separate refrigerant gas from liquid, ensuring smooth flow.</p><p>2. **Diaphragm Accumulators** employ a flexible diaphragm to achieve similar separation, suitable for smaller systems.</p><p>3. **Bladder Accumulators** contain a rubber bladder that expands to absorb excess liquid refrigerant, providing reliable performance.</p><p>4. **Others** encompass various designs suited for specific applications, offering versatile solutions in refrigeration systems.</p></p>
